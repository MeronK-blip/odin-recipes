Chicken Tortellini

Description

Chicken Cheese Tortellini is a simple pasta dish made with tender cheese-filled tortellini and pieces of cooked, seasoned chicken. The dish is combined with a cream, tomato sauce, garlic, \ and herbs, allowing the ingredients to blend together without overpowering their individual flavors. Itâ€™s straightforward to prepare and works well as a balanced, filling main course.

Ingredients

  • 4 thick-cut bacon slices
  • 2 (8 ounce) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, pounded to 1 inch thickness
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons kosher salt, divide
  • 3/4 cup drained julienne-cut sundried tomatoes in oil with herbs
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped shallot
  • 4 large garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup lower-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 (20 ounce) package refrigerated cheese tortellini
  • 1/2 cup pre-grated Parmesan cheese, plus more for garnish
  • torn fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Fill a large saucepan with salted water and bring to a boil over high heat.
  2. Meanwhile, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add chopped bacon; cook, stirring occasionally, until rendered and crispy, about 8 minutes.
  3. Using a slotted spoon, transfer bacon to a paper towel-lined plate to drain, reserving about 3 tablespoons drippings in skillet.
  4. Sprinkle chicken evenly with pepper and 1/4 teaspoon of the salt.
  5. Add chicken to skillet with drippings; cook over medium-high, until chicken is browned and cooked through, 5 to 6 minutes per side.
  6. Transfer chicken to a plate to cool and then chop chicken into bite-size pieces.
  7. Reduce heat to medium, and add sundried tomatoes and shallot; cook, stirring often, until softened, about 2 minutes.
  8. Add garlic, Italian seasoning, crushed red pepper, and remaining 1 teaspoon salt; cook, stirring constantly, until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  9. Add tomato paste; cook, stirring constantly, until deepened in color, about 1 minute.
  10. Pour in chicken broth; cook, stirring and scraping constantly to deglaze the skillet, about 1 minute.
  11. Stir in cream and bring to a simmer over medium; simmer stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened, 3 to 5 minutes.
  12. Add tortellini to boiling water, and boil until tortellini are tender and float to the surface, 3 to 5 minutes.
  13. Reserve 1 cup cooking water. Drain.
  14. Stir Parmesan cheese, reserved cooked bacon, reserved chopped chicken, and tortellini into skillet with cream mixture; cook, stirring and tossing constantly, until evenly coated in sauce.
  15. Add reserved cooking water, 1/4 cup at a time, if necessary to maintain a creamy consistency.
  16. Put mixture into a bowl; garnish with additional Parmesan cheese and a generous scattering of fresh basil.
